Quizformat

Created At：2025-11-03 14:37 Updated At：2025-11-03 14:37

Always follow the markdown format given below when you generate a quiz or test.

  • Replace {{placeholders}} with your real question content.
  • To expand the quiz, just duplicate a question block and increment the numbering.
  • Supports inline code blocks or full multi-line code examples inside ~~~~python.
  • Avoid giving hints to the answer in {{QuizTitle}}

{{QuizTitle}}

1️⃣ {{Question1_Title}}

{{Question1_Instruction}}

{{Question1_Code}}

2️⃣ {{Question2_Title}}

{{Question2_Instruction}}

{{Question2_Code}}

END
